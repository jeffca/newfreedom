Our Mission

Our mission is to join God in building a community that is becoming transformed and freed to love and serve as Jesus does, bringing reconciliation and healing to the world.
(2 Corinthians 3:16-18; 2 Corinthians 5:17-21; Galatians 5:1,13)

Our Vision

Our vision is to see people turning to God, hearts and relationships healed and neighborhoods blessed.
(Isaiah 61:1-3; John 8:34-36; John 10:10)

Our Values

Living for God's Glory (Isaiah 43:7)
Obeying God’s Word (2 Timothy 3:16-17)
Growing In Jesus’ Character in a Loving, Gracious, Truthful community (John 13:34-35)
Believing God for Redemption and Healing (Isaiah 53:4-5)
Love-driven, Spirit-empowered, Local-Global Witnessing (Matthew 5:13-16)
Building Christ-Honoring Relationships and Families (Malachi 4:6)

Who We Are

We are pilgrims on a spiritual journey to God, ordinary and broken people becoming transformed, healed and free in Jesus to love and serve as He does. Our calling is to follow Jesus to bless and make disciples of the people in our circles, neighborhoods and spheres.
